## Potential Questions & Answers

**Q: Is this a diagnostic tool?**
A: No, this is a screening tool only. It helps prioritize care and provides risk assessments. All recommendations emphasize consulting healthcare professionals.

**Q: How accurate is it?**
A: We use pre-trained models and comprehensive symptom analysis. However, we're clear this is for screening, not diagnosis. The tool is designed to support, not replace, medical judgment.

**Q: What about data privacy?**
A: We don't store any personal data. Images are processed temporarily and not saved. All processing can be done locally or on secure cloud infrastructure.

**Q: Can it work offline?**
A: The model can be downloaded and run locally, making it suitable for areas with limited internet connectivity.

**Q: What languages does it support?**
A: Currently English, but we've designed it to easily expand to Zulu, Xhosa, and Afrikaans in the future.

**Q: How do you ensure ethical AI?**
A: Clear disclaimers, transparent decision-making, no data storage, and emphasis on supporting rather than replacing healthcare professionals.
